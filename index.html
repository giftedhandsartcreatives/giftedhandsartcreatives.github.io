About Us

At Gifted Hands Art Creatives, we specialize in offering a curated selection of beautiful, hand made crafts, home deco, wall hangings and training services for everyone. Whether you’re looking to enhance your home decor, elevate a special event, or find the perfect gift, we provide timeless pieces that blend style and function. We make our crafts with the help of skilled artisans and trusted workers, focusing on craftsmanship, durability, and design. Our collection includes a wide range of styles african,modern and contemporaly styles In everything we do, our goal is simple: to help you bring beauty,joy and identity into your everyday life . We believe that the right craft is more than just a container. It’s a centerpiece, a conversation starter, and a reflection of your personal style.

What began as a small passion for beautiful home accents has grown into a carefully curated collection of craftwork that celebrate beauty,joy, creativity, and elegance that bring a sense of identity and belonging.we offer unique designs in different shapes and sizes that bring life into your house interior.We are dedicated to helping you create a home that feels beautiful,joyful and elegant. Whether you're styling your living room, searching for a thoughtful gift, or adding a touch of beauty to your workspace, we’re here to help you find the perfect piece. Thank you for being part of our journey.

Our Services

House Interior Decorations

We transform ordinary spaces into elegant, inviting homes. From concept to final touch, our interior decoration service blends functionality with your unique style, helping you create living areas that feel both beautiful and personal. Whether you’re refreshing a room or designing from scratch, we tailor each detail to suit your vision.

Moulding Decorative and Functional Vases

Our handcrafted vases are more than decor, they’re expressions of artistry and purpose. We design and mould both decorative and functional vases that complement a variety of interiors. Made with care and quality materials, each piece is unique, timeless, and built to last.

Wall Hangings

Bring your walls to life with handcrafted wall hangings that add texture, color, and character. From modern minimalist designs to bold artistic statements, our pieces are perfect for adding a personal touch to any room, ideal for home, office, or gifting.

Chrocheting

We create cozy, stylish crocheted items that blend tradition with modern flair. Whether it’s decorative pieces, accessories, or small home items, our crochet work is crafted with skill and passion. Each stitch carries warmth, charm, and handmade authenticity.

Training Services

Want to learn how to create beauty with your own hands? We offer hands-on training in pottery,weaveing,fabric design,jewererlly,crocheting, and decorative arts. Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, our training sessions are practical, inspiring, and tailored to help you grow your creative confidence.
